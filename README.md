**SmartPath** is more than just a task manager. It is an intelligent system designed to build the perfect rhythm for your life, where high academic achievement and personal goals harmoniously coexist with quality rest. 

We’ve built an environment that handles the routine of planning, so you can focus on what truly matters.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **📅 Intelligent Daily Scheduling** Forget the chaos. SmartPath automatically generates an optimal daily plan by seamlessly adapting to your academic timetable and prioritizing non-negotiable tasks.

* **🧠 Proactive AI Assistant** Unsure of your next move? The built-in digital mentor analyzes your current workload and tells you exactly **what** to do right now and **when** to start the next phase.

* **🎯 Smart Task Classification** Cut through the noise. The app helps you distinguish between what is truly important, what is urgent, and what can wait, ensuring your energy is spent where it counts.

* **⏱ Deep Focus Mode (Pomodoro)** Beat procrastination with an integrated Pomodoro timer. Break complex projects into manageable work intervals and short breaks to maintain maximum concentration.

* **🔔 Proactive Notifications** Never let a deadline or exam catch you off guard. Our notification system keeps you one step ahead by reminding you of critical events well in advance.

* **📈 Time Management Insights** SmartPath analyzes your workflow and provides personalized recommendations to help you master your efficiency and achieve more with less effort.

* **🧘‍♂️ Work-Life Balance** Productivity shouldn't lead to burnout. Our algorithms ensure that your pursuit of success always leaves room for rest, hobbies, and recovery.
